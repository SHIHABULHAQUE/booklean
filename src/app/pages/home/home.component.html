Don't Fix Your Finances

FTA Penalties & Fines

Late VAT filings carry penalties from AED 1,000 upward per period. Corporate Tax errors trigger much larger fines. One missed deadline with the FTA can cost more than a year of professional accounting fees.

Missed Funding & Investment

Banks and investors require clean, audited financials before they approve loans or back your business. Messy books mean missed opportunities — or worse, deals falling through at the last minute.

Cashflow Problems

Without real-time financial visibility, you can't see cashflow problems coming. Businesses that look profitable on paper run out of cash — not because they failed, but because nobody was watching the numbers.
